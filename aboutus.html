About Us

Welcome to Lansub Technologies Private Limited, where we revolutionize industrial operations with cutting-edge AI-driven solutions. Founded with the vision to enhance operational productivity, Lansub is dedicated to transforming the way businesses manage manufacturing processes, data integration, and decision-making.

Our flagship products, Opfact and G.A.P (Generative AI as Personally), are designed to address the critical challenges faced by industries today. Opfact is an AI-powered Manufacturing Execution System (MES) that integrates real-time machine data with ERP and CRM systems, offering predictive maintenance, AI-driven visual inspections, and resource optimization. G.A.P complements this by serving as a personalized AI assistant, processing diverse data formats like PDFs, images, and SQL to generate actionable insights in real time.

We also develop custom hardware solutions powered by NVIDIA Jetson, enabling seamless data capture from machines and secure cloud integration for real-time monitoring and analytics. This end-to-end ecosystem empowers businesses to optimize workflows, enhance productivity, and make data-driven decisions.

At Lansub, we are driven by innovation, scalability, and a commitment to helping businesses achieve their operational goals. Join us in shaping the future of industrial automation and AI-driven solutions.
